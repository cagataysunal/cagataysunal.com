Every developer has their own carefully curated toolkit. Here's what works for me after years of experimenting.

## Editor

I use a modern code editor with a minimal UI. The key is having powerful search and navigation features—you'll spend a lot of time jumping between files.

## Terminal

A good terminal setup can dramatically improve your productivity. Invest time in learning your shell properly.

## Version Control

Git is essential. Understanding branching strategies and commit hygiene goes a long way in collaborative projects.

The best tool is the one you're comfortable with. Experiment, iterate, and find your flow.
